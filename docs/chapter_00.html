The door opened up creakingly. Two shadowy figures accompanying a boy into the large room. There were three mysterious figures sitting on their own thrones who seems to be the ones in charge of this weird place. Suddenly, one of them speaks.


"Damon Marcellus, You are guilty"

"What!? I did nothing wrong! I'm innocent!"

The boy had flashbacks about the time before he got accused guilty.

"Damon! I fucking told you to clean the attic. Better do it right now before your bed sleeps with the flames!"

Damon panicked and quickly ran upstairs.

"Alright Alright! I'll do it. Just don't burn my comfy bed please"

Damon opened the dusty attic door that comes with a ladder. Using the feather duster, he cleaned up the place lazily and stacked the boxes. Something caught in his eye as he moved the boxes. He pulled out something from a box. It was wrapped in tattered cloth. For some reason it took him awhile to tear it off. Finally, it was uncovered. An old pocket watch with a weird design.


"Sheesh, why did I even want to check it in the first place. What a junk"
