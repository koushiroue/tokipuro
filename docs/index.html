A very early not-yet-a-proof-of-concept work-in-progress RPG Time-Manipulation Game.

Synopsis


Time itself is a fragile unexplained entropy of multiverses and sequences of events, through the discord of time; Time itself is balanced. However what happens if a human incidentally encounters a magic entity that could alter and manipulate the continuity of time. An all powerful demon is now unleashed and now time itself is now in shambles and if shattered, the universe will engage a full cataclysmic apocalypse... it's that human's job to stop the universal apocalypse and vanquish the demon with his strange newfound powers through a magic pocket watch.
